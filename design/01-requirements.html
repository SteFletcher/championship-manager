01 · Requirements Specification

Software Requirements Specification (SRS) — functional & non-functional

1. Purpose & Scope

Champman is a single-player football-management career game that runs entirely in the browser. The player assumes the role of a manager at one of 40 real 1995/96 clubs and is responsible for squad selection, tactics, transfers, finances, stadium development, and meeting board expectations across full seasons of league and cup football.

In scope
  • Career mode with a single human-managed club and 23 AI-managed clubs.
  • Two 20-club divisions with promotion/relegation and a 40-club knockout cup.
  • Resumable minute-by-minute match simulation with live tactical management.
  • Squad, tactics, transfers, finances, scouting, stadium, board, and news systems.
  • Deterministic save/load to localStorage.
Out of scope (deferred)
  • European competition, internationals, and hotseat multiplayer.
  • Backend services, accounts, cross-device sync.
  • Audio, 3D graphics, or licensed real-world club/player data.

3. Functional Requirements

Requirements are grouped by subsystem. Each carries an ID for traceability into the architecture and test plan.

3.1 Career & World (CAR)

IDRequirementAcceptance criterion
CAR-01The player shall choose a manager name and one of 40 clubs to begin a career.New game places the chosen club under human control; all others AI.
CAR-02The world shall contain two divisions of 20 clubs with deterministic squads.40 clubs; same club always yields the same 18 players across new games.
CAR-03The season shall be a calendar of league rounds interleaved with cup rounds.38 league rounds + 6 cup days = 44 calendar weeks for a 20-club division.
CAR-04Two clubs shall be promoted and two relegated each season.Top 2 of Division 2 swap divisions with bottom 2 of Division 1.
CAR-05Board confidence shall track performance and trigger sacking below a threshold.Confidence < 15 → manager sacked; job market opens.
CAR-06A sacked manager shall be able to take a job at a lesser club.Up to 2 reachable clubs offered; accepting continues the career mid-season.
CAR-07Strong seasons shall attract offers from bigger clubs.Poach offer raised when reputation exceeds current club tier + 5.
CAR-08Manager reputation shall move with seasons, trophies, and sackings.Champion +12, cup win +8, promotion +8, relegation −8, sack −12.

3.2 Match Simulation (MAT)

IDRequirementAcceptance criterion
MAT-01The match engine shall simulate minute-by-minute and be pausable at any time.MatchSim.playMinute() advances one minute; pauseMatch() halts the timer.
MAT-02The same seed shall produce an identical match.Deep-equal result objects across replays with the same seed.
MAT-03The engine shall model possession, chances, shots, goals, fouls, cards, and injuries.Score equals goal events; stats recorded per side and per player.
MAT-04Up to 3 substitutions per side; sent-off players never reappear.subsUsed ≤ 3; sent-off set membership prevents return.
MAT-05The match shall auto-pause at half time, extra time, red cards, and injuries to the user's players.Key-moment banner shown; user left a man short until reacting to own injuries.
MAT-06Formation and mentality shall be changeable mid-match by both user and AI.setTactics() remaps slots; AI chases when behind, shuts up shop when ahead.
MAT-07Knockout ties level after 90 minutes shall go to extra time then penalties.30 min ET; best-of-5 then sudden-death shootout.
MAT-08Per-player live ratings out of 10 shall update from match events.Goals +1.1, assists +0.6, saves +0.18, red −1.3, clean sheet +0.5, etc.
MAT-09Home advantage shall apply to the home side.1.12 multiplier on home midfield/attack effectiveness.

3.3 Squad & Players (SQD)

IDRequirementAcceptance criterion
SQD-01Each club shall carry an 18-player squad with position, age, attributes, and hidden traits.2 GK, 6 DF, 6 MF, 4 FW; consistency & injuryProne hidden.
SQD-02Players shall have condition, form, morale, wages, value, and contract years.All fields present and updated weekly.
SQD-03The engine shall auto-select the best available XI + 5-man bench for a formation.selectXI() deterministic; injured/suspended never picked.
SQD-04Out-of-position players shall incur a penalty.0.5× for GK mismatches, 0.75× otherwise.
SQD-05Players shall develop at season end: youngsters grow, veterans fade, some retire.≤21 grow +4; >31 fade −4; age ≥34 retires at 60% chance.
SQD-06Injuries shall carry real durations; 5 yellows or a red bring bans.injuryWeeks decremented weekly; banMatches ticks down on sit-outs.
SQD-07A youth intake shall keep squads viable.Squads refilled to ≥16 each season end.

3.4 Tactics (TAC)

IDRequirementAcceptance criterion
TAC-01Five formations and three mentalities shall be selectable.4-4-2, 4-3-3, 4-5-1, 3-5-2, 5-3-2; defensive/normal/attacking.
TAC-02The manager shall swap players between XI, bench, and reserves by clicking.Two clicks complete a swap; lineup persists until invalid.
TAC-03Mentality shall affect chance creation and concession rates.Attacking ×1.12 create / ×1.08 concede; defensive ×0.85 / ×0.90.

3.5 Transfers & Scouting (TRF)

IDRequirementAcceptance criterion
TRF-01The manager shall search the market with position and value filters.searchPlayers() returns ranked rows excluding own club.
TRF-02Bids shall be evaluated as accepted / countered / rejected based on asking price.≥ asking → accepted; ≥ 90% value → countered at midpoint; else rejected.
TRF-03Players shall have willingness that depends on the tier step.playerAgrees() reluctant to drop divisions.
TRF-04Squad-protection rules shall prevent selling below a floor.≥ 15 players and ≥ 2 GKs always retained.
TRF-05AI clubs shall bid for the user's players and conduct AI-to-AI transfers.Pending offers arrive in inbox; ~15% weekly AI-to-AI chance.
TRF-06Free agents shall be signable without a fee.#signFreeAgent path with wage demand only.
TRF-07The manager shall transfer-list and renew contracts.Listed players cheaper to buy (0.85×) and draw more AI interest.
TRF-08Other clubs' attributes shall show as estimate ranges until scouted.£15k scout report (1 week) reveals exact attributes; ranges stable & truth-containing.
TRF-09Pre-match opposition reports shall show form, predicted XI, and danger man.oppositionReport() returns XI + form guide + danger man.

3.6 Finances & Stadium (FIN)

IDRequirementAcceptance criterion
FIN-01Each club shall have a balance, transfer budget, and weekly wage bill.Wages deducted weekly; transfer budget ring-fenced from balance.
FIN-02Home gate receipts shall follow attendance, capped by stadium capacity.attendance × £14 added to home balance weekly.
FIN-03A fanbase shall grow with wins and drain with defeats.×1.008 win, ×1.001 draw, ×0.996 loss; floor 2,000.
FIN-04The manager shall request stadium expansion when demand warrants.Fanbase ≥ 85% capacity & affordable → 8-week build, +15% seats.
FIN-05AI boards shall expand their grounds when bursting.AI expands at 110% fanbase/capacity with 1.5× cost in reserve.
FIN-06Prize money shall be paid at season end by finishing position.Champion of Div 1 receives £500k bonus + positional base.

3.7 News & Awards (NWS)

IDRequirementAcceptance criterion
NWS-01An inbox shall receive time-stamped news items with optional actions.Items capped at 120; offer items carry accept/reject buttons.
NWS-02Monthly Player of the Month and end-of-season awards shall be issued.PotM requires ≥ 2 appearances in the month; season awards include golden boot & player of season.

3.8 Persistence (SAV)

IDRequirementAcceptance criterion
SAV-01The game shall auto-save to localStorage after every week.Save written on advanceWeek and after transfers/tactics.
SAV-02A saved game shall restore and continue deterministically.RNG state restored; resumed career replays identically.
SAV-03The save format shall carry a version for compatibility gating.SAVE_VERSION 9; additive migrations upgrade v6–v8 saves; restore throws on mismatch.

4. Non-Functional Requirements

CategoryRequirementTarget / measure
PerformanceEngine-only operations complete without perceptible delay.A full season sim headless completes in < 1 s.
PortabilityNo build step; runs from any static file server.npm run serve (python http.server) suffices.
DeterminismAll randomness flows through one seeded RNG.Identical seed → identical career (tested).
TestabilityEngine is pure & framework-free; UI is a thin client.213 node:test tests, no DOM dependency in engine.
AccessibilityKeyboard navigability and ARIA labelling on key controls.Team rows focusable + Enter/Space; aria-live on commentary.
FootprintZero runtime dependencies.package.json devDependencies only.
RealismStatistical distributions over thousands of matches are believable.~1.3–2.4 goals/match; home advantage present; mismatches dominate.

5. Constraints & Assumptions

  • Single-player only. No network multiplayer or shared state.
  • Real data. Clubs and some players use real 1995/96 data.
  • Browser-local storage. Saves do not leave the device; clearing storage loses the career.
  • ES module browser target. Requires a browser supporting native ES modules (all evergreen browsers).
  • One human club. The other 23 clubs are always AI-managed; there is no hotseat mode.
